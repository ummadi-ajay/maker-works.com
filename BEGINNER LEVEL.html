Maker Works Junior Program

A fun-filled 2-year creative learning program for curious minds aged 7–10

📥 Enroll Now
👧 Age Group

Ages 7–10 years

📆 Duration

2 Years (Weekend-Only Program)

🎨 Sessions

8 Creative Activities Per Month

🎉 What They'll Explore

In this playful program, kids dive into science and art through fun activities — like building simple machines, making creative art projects, exploring colors, light, and shapes, and trying out basic robotics kits that are safe and exciting for little innovators.

🌟 Why Join the Junior Program?

Fun Learning

We use games, crafts, and playful tools to teach exciting topics in a joyful way.

Friendly Mentors

Our kind and caring teachers guide kids patiently through each activity and game.

Safe & Supportive

We ensure a safe and happy space where kids feel confident to express themselves.

Team Activities

Children collaborate and celebrate creativity by working on team mini-projects.

Skills Students Develop

Curiosity & Creativity

Children learn to ask questions, explore, and invent their own fun solutions.

Art & Imagination

We blend art into science to let imagination run wild with colorful projects.

Teamwork

Kids work in small groups to learn collaboration, sharing, and leadership.
