MakerWorks Portfolio Building Program

A focused 1-year program to help students build strong tech + design portfolios

📥 Join the Program
🎯 Target Group

Students aged 13–17 (Grades 8–12)

⏳ Duration

1 Year (Weekend + Project Support)

📁 Final Outcome

Personal Portfolio + 3 Major Projects

🛠️ What They'll Build

Students work on real-world challenges using coding, electronics, 3D modeling, and web design. By the end, they’ll have a digital portfolio website, 2–3 impressive projects, and presentation-ready documentation for college, scholarships, or competitions.

🌟 Why Choose This Program?

Personalized Mentorship

One-on-one project guidance with tech and design mentors throughout the year.

Portfolio Website

Students build a personal portfolio site to showcase skills and documented projects.

Project Showcase

Participate in review days where projects are evaluated by professionals and peers.

College & Career Ready

Gain a strong head-start for applications to STEAM-based higher studies or jobs.

Skills Students Develop

Full-Stack Thinking

From frontend design to backend logic, students get holistic technical exposure.

Design & UX

Learn how to present ideas beautifully through clean, user-friendly interfaces.

Documentation

Students write their own project logs, reflections, and pitches to build confidence.
