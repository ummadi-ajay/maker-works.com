An exciting 3-year journey into hands-on innovation for ages 11–14

📥 Enroll Now
👥 Age Group

Ages 11–14 years

📆 Duration

3 Years (Weekend-Only Program)

🔄 Sessions

8 to 10 Hands-on Sessions Per Month

🔧 What They'll Learn

This program is the perfect launchpad for young minds to explore STEAM (Science, Technology, Engineering, Arts, and Math). Each session is built to spark curiosity through hands-on activities like building robots, creating circuits, experimenting with 3D printing, and crafting simple machines using recycled materials.

🌟 Why Choose This Program?

Hands-On Learning

Kids build projects and learn by doing — from circuit boards to cardboard engineering.

Creative Thinking

Our activities spark imagination through art, design, storytelling, and invention games.

Expert Mentors

Guided by passionate mentors with real-world experience in making, design, and education.

Teamwork & Fun

Children work in teams to complete missions and present their creations proudly.

Skills Students Develop

Critical Thinking

Projects are designed to challenge young minds to think logically and solve problems creatively.

Coding & Computational Logic

Students gain a strong foundation in block-based and text-based programming through engaging tools.

Hands-On Making

From building circuits to 3D models, students experience the joy of making with real tools and materials.

Teamwork & Collaboration

Interactive group tasks help kids learn to communicate, share responsibilities, and work together.

Innovation & Creativity

We foster out-of-the-box thinking and help kids take their ideas from imagination to reality.

Confidence & Leadership

Presenting projects and taking initiative builds confidence and leadership from a young age.
